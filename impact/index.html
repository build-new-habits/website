How it works

Five percent of everything Alongside earns goes to causes our community believes in. Each quarter, three causes are selected — each one representing something Alongside stands for: dignity, support, and our connection to the world around us.

Your credits are your voice in where that money goes. You can split them, give them all to one cause, or carry them forward and watch them grow until a cause moves you enough to commit.

The commitment

Five percent is where we start. Not where we stop.
As Alongside grows, so does the commitment.
That's not a promise we made to investors. It's a promise we made to you.

Past allocations

Transparency matters. Every quarter, we publish where the credits went, how much was allocated, and what it funded. You can see every decision we've made since day one.
